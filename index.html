About CTF

Capture The Flags, or CTFs, are a kind of computer security competition. Jeopardy CTFs are the most common kind of CTF. Attack & Defense CTFs are a less common kind of CTF with more moving parts . They're rarely done for the general public because of their complexity. Jeopardy CTFsrevolve around a set of challenges which are provided by competition organizers to competitors. Competitors form teams and then work on the challenges together. Each challenge is designed so that when the competitor solves it, a small piece of text or "ag" is revealed. The ag is then submitted to a website or scoring engine in exchange for points. The amount of points rewarded is typically relative to the perceived diculty of the challenge.
